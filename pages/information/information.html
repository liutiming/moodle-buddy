Moodle Buddy
How to use?
  1. Log into your university's Moodle system
  2. Visit the main page of a Moodle course of your choice
  3. Click on the Moodle Buddy logo in the extension bar of your browser
  4. Explore all the features Moodle Buddy has to offer
Why?

Normally, a student would have to search the Moodle course website for all downloadable resources and download them all one by one. This takes a lot of time and is a very annoying, redundant and mundane task.

This browser plugin saves TU students a lot of time as they now can download all resources with ease of just one click.

Who am I?

I am an Information Systems Engineering student at TU Berlin who is sometimes really annoyed by the Moodle platform ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

This plugin is NOT officially associated with the Moodle!

Privacy

Of course I do not have access to any login data! The resources are ONLY downloaded to YOUR computer! I do NOT collect the downloaded resources in any way or form!
